FAQs

How do I add a new user?
Go to Users and click Add User in Quick Actions. Fill in the details and submit.
How can I export attendance data?
Use the Export button in the top bar. Select your format and date range.
What if a device is offline?
Check power and network. If unresolved, contact IT or see the Device Guide.
How do I change my settings?
Go to Settings in the sidebar, update info, and click Save Changes.
Who do I contact for support?
Email support@biodash.com or use the form below.
